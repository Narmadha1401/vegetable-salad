VEGETABLE SALAD


BENEFITS OF THE VEGETABLE SALAD

Aside from their natural good taste and great crunchy texture alongside wonderful colours and fraganace,eating a large serving of fresh,raw veggies each day can have significant health benefits.

STEPS TO MAKE VEGEATBLE SALAD

  • Take a large and clear glass bowl.
  • Try adding blanched,lightly stemaed or roasted veggies like bronchi,french beans,corn,cauliflower,babycorn.
  • Add lentils,beans,or chickpeas or bean sprouts for more proteins.
  • Swap in your own favourite combination of dried friuts,nuts,and seeds.
  • Use fresh coriandor or chives instead of parsely.
